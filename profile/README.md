**AIX Group** is a Tier-1 AI consultancy delivering production-grade Artificial Intelligence, Large Language Model integration, and cloud-native architecture to enterprise clients worldwide. We operate at the intersection of deep research and real-world engineering — translating cutting-edge AI capabilities into reliable, scalable, and auditable business systems.

Our engagements span **Insurance, Energy, Fintech, and Healthcare** — industries where precision, security, and uptime are non-negotiable. We don't build demos. We build systems that run at scale.
